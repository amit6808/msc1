INDIAN MEDICAL ASSOCIATION


Not only doctors, Pharmacists too have a role in ethical dispensing of prescription medicines. Safe and effective use of medicines is a complementary effort. MCI Code of Medical Ethics ( Indian Medical CouncilI (Professional conduct, Etiquette and Ethics) Regulations, 2002 and subsequent amendments) has some provisions in it that are of relevance to the pharmacists.
